About

Clarity, Fit, and Confidentiality

Branta Partners is a buy-side advisory firm that works exclusively with established trades and home services companies. Selling a business you've built with your own hands is deeply personal, and our role is to make discreet, well-matched introductions, not run a broad public process.

Why Trades Owners Choose Us

  • Trades expertise. We understand service quality standards and field operations.
  • Efficient path. We introduce only well-fitted investors who value quality craftsmanship.
  • Privacy-first support. We guide preparation while maintaining customer confidentiality.

What "Buy-Side" Means for Trades

  • Values-aligned partners. Investors share commitment to service quality and operational excellence.
  • You stay in control. We facilitate introductions; you drive terms and timing.
  • Confidential process. Only curated fits, no broad marketing or public listings.
